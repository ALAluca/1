Abdulrahman "Luca" Alihassan

Alihassan L. Abdulrahman (born 22 September 2001), better known as Luca, is a Filipino professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player for Natus Vincere. He is considered to be one of the best players in Global Offensive history. I specialize in Front-end, Responsive Web Development, and UI/UX Design.
